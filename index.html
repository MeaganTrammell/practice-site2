A little about me

I'm a writer of dark speculative fiction, specifically fantasy and science fiction. I earned my BFA in Creative Writing from George Mason University in 2019 and my MFA from the same university in 2023. As of yet I have no published works, but my thesis is available for viewing as well as a few writing samples you can view here.
